ATV Trails

There are hundreds of miles of ATV trails along with the many snowmobile trails and game trails in the area. The ATV trails will take you through many conditions such as sand dunes, swamps, streams and deep bush trails. There are also many open air trails that go for many miles. There is a halfday loop through the Chapleau Game Preserve. And another halfday loop west of there.

Want a longer ride? If you have a GPS, you can take the snowmobile trail to Dubreiville and back.

The really great advantage to having an ATV at our camp is you will have access to lots of lakes that can only be accessed by ATV. Many of these lakes are seldom fished. As a result you can catch fish right from shore or bring a canoe if you have a boat rack. ATVers can bring the inflatable fishing tubes with a pair of flippers and spend the afternoon floating around and fishing. We have a great knowledge of the local lakes and can direct you to a few that will produce great fishing.
